Cheesecake

Description

"Cheesecake is a creamy, rich dessert with a smooth texture and a buttery crust, often topped with fruits, sauces, or other sweet toppings."

Ingredients

  • 200g digestive biscuits, crushed
  • 100g unsalted butter, melted
  • 500g cream cheese, softened
  • 200g granulated sugar
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 200ml sour cream
  • 150ml heavy cream
  • Fresh berries or fruit compote (optional, for topping)

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 160°C (320°F) and grease a springform pan.
  2. Mix the crushed digestive biscuits with the melted butter and press the mixture firmly into the bottom of the pan to form the crust.
  3. Bake the crust for 10 minutes, then remove it from the oven and let it cool.
  4. In a large bowl, beat the cream cheese and sugar together until smooth and creamy.
  5. Add the eggs one at a time, mixing well after each addition, then stir in the vanilla extract.
  6. Blend in the sour cream and heavy cream until the mixture is well combined and smooth.
  7. Pour the cheesecake batter over the cooled crust and smooth the top.
  8. Bake for 50-60 minutes or until the center is just set but still slightly jiggly.
  9. Turn off the oven, crack the door open, and let the cheesecake cool gradually for 1 hour.
  10. Chill the cheesecake in the refrigerator for at least 4 hours or overnight before serving.
  11. Top with fresh berries or fruit compote, if desired, before slicing and serving.

you can also use some creams for top of your cheesecake, like lotus cream or any other cream that you can buy from market.
